Counseling FAQs

Common questions about individual counseling

How long does each counseling session last?

Each individual counseling session lasts 50 minutes. The initial consultation may extend to 60 minutes to allow for comprehensive assessment.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies based on individual needs. Some clients see progress in 4-6 sessions, while others may benefit from longer-term therapy. We'll discuss this during your initial consultation.

Is online counseling available?

Yes, we offer both in-person sessions at our Coimbatore center and secure online counseling via video conferencing for your convenience.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand that schedules can change. You can reschedule or cancel your session with 24 hours notice without any penalty.
