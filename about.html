Meet R.C. Nandhini

Founder & Mental Health Counsellor

With over 5 years of dedicated experience in mental health counseling, R.C. Nandhini founded THULIR.IDAM with a vision to create a safe haven for emotional healing in Coimbatore. Her journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to compassionate, professional mental health support.

Having completed her Master's in Psychology and multiple certifications in counseling and therapy, R.C. Nandhini combines academic excellence with practical experience to provide holistic care to her clients.

Our Therapeutic Approach

At THULIR.IDAM, we believe in a client-centered approach that combines evidence-based therapeutic techniques with compassionate care.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors

Person-Centered Therapy

Creating a supportive environment for self-exploration and growth

Solution-Focused Brief Therapy

Focusing on solutions and future possibilities rather than problems

Mindfulness-Based Techniques

Incorporating mindfulness practices for emotional regulation
