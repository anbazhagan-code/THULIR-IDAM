About Thulir.Idam

Thulir.Idam is a safe and supportive space where you can pause, heal and grow. Created for people dealing with everyday challenges like stress, overthinking, emotional struggles, relationship concerns, or simply feeling overwhelmed. Here, you don’t have to worry about being judged or labelled. The focus is on helping you understand yourself better, feel more balanced, and handle life with more clarity and confidence.

Nandhini Rajendran

Mental Health Counsellor & Emotional Wellness Facilitator | Founder & Director

I work with students and professionals navigating stress, overthinking, emotional challenges, and phases of feeling stuck. My aim is to create a safe, comfortable space where you can talk openly and understand yourself better. Along with one-on-one counselling, I facilitate emotional well-being sessions in schools, colleges & organizations — helping people build awareness, manage emotions, and handle everyday challenges in a healthier way. My approach is person-centered, supporting you in a way that works best for you.
